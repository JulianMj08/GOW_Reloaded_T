About Reloaded

Reloaded Tournaments was created with one clear goal: to bring life back to the competitive Gears of War Reloaded scene in Europe.

We believe the community needs more than public matches. It needs goals, healthy rivalry, tournaments and real reasons to compete. This project was created by players, for players, with the intention of offering fair, well-organized tournaments with real prizes.

It doesn't matter if a tournament starts with 4 teams or 16: if there are teams ready to compete, the tournament will happen. We want every event to be a real opportunity to compete, improve and prove your level.

We are starting progressively, step by step. As the platform grows and the community responds, our goal is to attract sponsors who allow us to increase prize pools and further improve the competitive experience.

Every tournament is designed to offer a serious and organized experience with active moderation, clear communication and the commitment to build a strong, competitive and long-lasting community around Gears of War.
