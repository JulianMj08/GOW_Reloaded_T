RELOADED TOURNAMENTS

Official Rulebook — Gears of War Reloaded (EU)

This document contains the official competitive regulations for all tournaments organized by Reloaded Tournaments | Gears EU. All participants are required to read and understand these rules before registering.

Failure to comply may result in penalties or disqualification.

1. GENERAL INFORMATION

1.1 Region

This tournament is open exclusively to players residing in the European Union.

1.2 Format

  • 4 vs 4
  • Double Elimination Bracket
  • Mode: Execution
  • Crossplay enabled (Maximum 2 PC players per team)
  • All series are Best of 3 (Bo3)
  • Semi-finals and Grand Final: First to 7 rounds per map
  • The official bracket will be published in the designated channel before the tournament begins.

2. TEAM ELIGIBILITY & ROSTERS

2.1 Team Composition

  • 4 starting players are mandatory.
  • 1 substitute allowed (maximum 5 registered players).
  • Teams may not compete with fewer than 4 players under any circumstances.

2.2 Registration Lock

All players must be registered at least 24 hours before the tournament start. Unregistered players are not allowed to participate.

2.3 Team Captain

The Team Captain is responsible for:

  • Communication with staff
  • Match reporting
  • Roster compliance

3. MATCH SETTINGS

3.1 Execution Settings

  • Rounds to Win: 5
  • Round Duration: 4 minutes
  • Bleed-Out Time: 10 seconds
  • Active Reload: Enabled
  • Self-Revive: Disabled
  • Friendly Fire: Disabled
  • Weapon Respawn: 1 minute
  • Weapon Substitution: Disabled
  • Weapon Settings: Default
  • Winning Condition: The team that reaches 5 rounds first wins the map.

4. MAP POOL

Available Maps:

  • Canals
  • Clocktower
  • Deadlock
  • Mansion
  • War Machine
  • Raven Down

Additional maps eligible for Decider Map:

  • Swamp
  • Gas Station
  • Process
  • Mausoleum
  • Rooftops
  • Sanctuary

(The final map pool may be adjusted before tournament start.)

5. MAP SELECTION & HOSTING

5.1 Map Selection

Map veto and selection details will be displayed on the match lobby page.

Standard procedure:

  • Team A hosts Map 1 and selects the map.
  • Team B hosts Map 2 and selects the map.
  • If a Map 3 is required:
    • The team with the best round differential across Maps 1 & 2 hosts.
    • The opposing team selects the Decider Map.
  • Round differential = total rounds won minus total rounds lost.

6. MATCH COMMUNICATION

All match coordination must be conducted via the official Discord server. Teams must join the designated channels when required.

7. MATCH START & FORFEIT POLICY

7.1 Late Arrival Rule

If a team cannot field 4 players within 15 minutes after the scheduled start time:

  • They automatically forfeit Map 1.
  • After Map 1 forfeit: An additional 10 minutes is allowed before each subsequent map.
  • If the team still cannot field 4 players: They forfeit that map.
  • This continues until:
    • The team fields 4 players, or
    • The series is lost.

These rules apply to all rounds, including Semi-finals and Grand Final. No exceptions.

8. MATCH RESULT REPORTING

To advance in the bracket, teams must submit:

  • A screenshot or video showing victory
  • Final score (example: 2-1)
  • Opponent team name

Results must be submitted within 15 minutes after match completion. Failure to report results may result in delays or penalties.

9. STREAMING POLICY

Launch Tournament Exception: Players may stream their matches during the launch event. Future Events: Streaming rights may be exclusive to the official tournament channel. Unauthorized streaming in future tournaments may result in penalties or disqualification.

10. CHARACTER RESTRICTIONS

Players may use any character. No character restrictions apply at this time.

11. COMPETITIVE INTEGRITY & CHEATING

Strict competitive integrity is enforced. The following are strictly prohibited:

  • Use of third-party cheat software
  • Aim assistance tools
  • Wallhacks or visual manipulation
  • Exploiting unintended game bugs
  • Lag switching
  • Network manipulation
  • Smurfing to bypass skill or region rules
  • Account sharing
  • Intentional match fixing
  • Boosting or collusion between teams

Any suspicion may lead to:

  • Immediate investigation
  • Request for gameplay recording
  • Temporary suspension pending review

Confirmed cheating results in:

  • Immediate disqualification
  • Permanent ban from future events

Staff decisions are final.

12. PENALTIES

Depending on severity:

  • Warning
  • Round loss
  • Map forfeit
  • Series forfeit
  • Disqualification
  • Temporary ban
  • Permanent ban

13. PRIZE DISTRIBUTION

All cash prizes will be distributed via Reloaded Tournaments. Winners will receive funds through their Reloaded Tournaments accounts and may withdraw via:

  • PayPal
  • Bank transfer (depending on availability)

Prize distribution may take several business days after tournament verification.

14. FINAL AUTHORITY

Reloaded Tournaments administration reserves the right to:

  • Modify rules before tournament start
  • Interpret unclear situations
  • Make final decisions in disputes

All administrative decisions are final.
